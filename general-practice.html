Your path to wellness through our expertise

The Department of General Medicine offers comprehensive diagnostic services and general medical care. Disease diagnosis, management, and prevention are the main priorities of this Department. It works closely with other specialized medical departments to ensure that all patient needs are identified and met.

What diseases does a General Practitioner deal with

  • We carry out vaccinations
  • We perform preventive examination (disease screenings)
  • We give recommendations on nutrition, physical activity, lifestyle
  • We carry out preventive examination of healthy children
  • We assess the risks of developing diseases

What kind of investigations are ordered by a General Practitioner

At the medical center Polyclinic.ae, there is a modern laboratory and functional diagnostics rooms, where, if necessary, you will be conducted:

  • ECG
  • Ultrasound of internal organs
  • CT, MRI of various organs
  • Radiography (chest organs, bones, facial skull, spine, joints, abdominal cavity, kidneys)
  • General clinical blood and urine tests, stool analysis, determination of vitamin D, culture for pneumococci, and antibacterial sensitivity
  • If you have a headache, rash, sore back, or increased body temperature, do not hesitate to ask for help at Polyclinic.ae

It is an integrated approach in the Department of Family Medicine Polyclinic.ae that helps preserve the physical and psychological health of patients.

A flexible schedule of appointments is suitable for patients with a busy work schedule, parents with children, and active individuals.

Comprehensive General Medicine Services in Dubai, including:

Preventive Care – Routine checkups, vaccinations, screenings.
Acute Care – Treatment for short-term illnesses and minor injuries.
Minor Procedures – Suturing, skin lesion removal, wound care.
Children’s Health – Pediatric care, immunizations, growth monitoring.
Women's Health – Pregnancy advice, contraception, screenings.
Geriatric Care – Senior health, fall prevention, medication reviews.
