Dr. Saeed Al-Khalifa

Internal Medicine Specialist

Profile

Dr. Saeed Al-Khalifa is an experienced Internal Medicine Specialist with over 40 years of medical practice. He graduated from Cairo University, Egypt, in 1980 and has since established himself as a respected figure in the field of internal medicine. Dr. Al-Khalifa has worked in numerous healthcare institutions across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and has been part of the Ministry of Health for over two decades, providing expert care to a wide range of patients. With extensive experience in diagnosing and managing complex medical conditions, Dr. Al-Khalifa specializes in chronic disease management, including cardiovascular diseases, diabetes, hypertension, and respiratory disorders. His comprehensive approach to patient care emphasizes not only the treatment of illness but also the prevention of disease and the promotion of overall health and well-being. Dr. Al-Khalifa is known for his deep knowledge of the intricacies of internal medicine, particularly in managing multifaceted medical conditions in elderly patients. His compassionate demeanor, combined with his vast clinical knowledge, has earned him the trust and respect of his patients and colleagues alike. Dr. Al-Khalifa remains an active participant in various medical conferences and continuously strives to stay updated with the latest advancements in medical science.

Dr. Amina Khaled

Consultant Internal Medicine

Profile

Dr. Amina Khaled is a highly skilled Consultant in Internal Medicine with over 10 years of clinical experience. She graduated from King Abdulaziz University, Jeddah, in 2011, and went on to complete advanced training in Internal Medicine, earning a reputation for her expertise in the management of both acute and chronic medical conditions. Dr. Khaled has worked in prominent medical institutions across Saudi Arabia, where she has honed her skills in treating complex medical cases with a focus on evidence-based practices. Her special interests include the management of chronic diseases such as diabetes, hypertension, and gastrointestinal disorders, as well as preventive healthcare to reduce the risk of developing serious conditions. Dr. Khaled is passionate about integrating the latest medical research with patient care, ensuring that her patients receive the most up-to-date and comprehensive treatment. Known for her excellent communication skills, Dr. Khaled takes a patient-centered approach to medicine, listening carefully to her patients' concerns and working closely with them to develop individualized treatment plans. Her dedication to continuous learning and professional development makes her a highly respected and trusted consultant in the field.
