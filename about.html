Services We Provide

SmartCare Polyclinic in Dubai offers a comprehensive range of medical services to meet the diverse healthcare needs of the community. Here are five key aspects of their offerings:

✓ General Practice: SmartCare provides primary healthcare services, addressing a wide array of health concerns and serving as the first point of contact for patients seeking medical attention.

✓ Specialized Medical Services: The clinic offers specialized care in areas such as Internal Medicine, Family Medicine, Pediatrics, and Gynecology, ensuring patients receive expert treatment for various health conditions.

✓ Dental Care: SmartCare includes a dedicated dental department, offering services ranging from routine check-ups to advanced dental procedures, promoting oral health and hygiene.

✓ Diagnostic Services: The clinic provides comprehensive pathology services, facilitating accurate diagnosis and effective treatment planning for patients.

✓ Home Healthcare: SmartCare extends its services beyond the clinic by offering home healthcare, ensuring patients receive necessary medical attention in the comfort of their homes.
