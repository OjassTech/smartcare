Our Services

SmartCare Polyclinic is committed to delivering exceptional and comprehensive healthcare services in Deira, Dubai. With a diverse range of medical specialties, we strive to meet the unique healthcare needs of our community. Our services include:

General Practice

Comprehensive healthcare for individuals of all ages, including routine check-ups, preventive care, and treatment for common ailments.
Family Medicine

Comprehensive healthcare for families, focusing on preventive care and management of chronic conditions.

Internal Medicine

Specialized care for adults, focusing on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of internal diseases.

Pediatrics

Comprehensive healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents, focusing on their unique health needs.

Gynecology

Specialized care for women's health, including routine exams, screenings, and reproductive health services.

Dentistry

Comprehensive dental care, including preventive, restorative, and cosmetic dentistry services.

Pathology

Diagnostic services to identify diseases through laboratory testing and analysis.

Home Healthcare

In-home medical care and support for patients with chronic illnesses or those recovering from surgery.
